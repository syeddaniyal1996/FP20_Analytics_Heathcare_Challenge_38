# FP20_Analytics_Heathcare_Challenge_38
Code Blue Healthcare Dashboard is a comprehensive Power BI solution built to analyze healthcare operations, clinical performance, workforce wellbeing, and financial sustainability across multiple hospitals.

The dashboard combines executive-level KPIs with operational and workforce insights, helping decision-makers identify performance bottlenecks, quality concerns, staffing challenges, and financial opportunities.

# Dashboard Pages
## 1. Executive Overview (Patient Flow & Volumes)

This page provides a high-level view of hospital performance.

### Key Metrics
Total Visits: 9,994
Average Treatment Delay: 73.99 minutes
Readmission Rate: 17.04%
Burnout Risk Score: 0.59
Financial Profit Margin: 25.73%

### Analysis

The page highlights the relationship between patient demand, workforce availability, and operational efficiency. Increased staff absences are associated with treatment delays and higher readmission rates.

### Visuals Included

Monthly Absence Trend Analysis
Regional Absence Comparison
Department Absence Distribution
Bed Occupancy vs Staffing Stress Analysis

## 2. Clinical Quality & Medical Outcomes

This page focuses on treatment effectiveness and patient outcomes.

### Key Analysis Areas

Mortality Rate by Department
Wait Time vs Patient Satisfaction
Patient Outcome Distribution
Operational Performance Metrics

### Insights

Departments with higher wait times tend to show reduced satisfaction scores. Mortality rate comparisons help identify clinical areas requiring additional attention and resource allocation.

### Visuals Included

Outcome Distribution Charts
Mortality Analysis
Satisfaction Correlation Analysis
Clinical Performance Matrix

## 3. Workforce Strain & Financial Sustainability

This page evaluates workforce wellbeing alongside financial performance.

### Key Metrics

Total Staff Cost: $30.77M
Staff Absences: 7.65K
Total Overtime: 39.19K Hours
Burnout Risk Score: 0.59
Profit Margin: 25.73%

### Insights

The analysis demonstrates how staff shortages and overtime contribute to workforce burnout. Hospitals maintaining balanced staffing levels and occupancy rates generally achieve better financial outcomes.
